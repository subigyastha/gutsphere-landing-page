## Persona 1: Uncertain Explorer

*"Something isn't right, but I don't know what's causing it."*

### Walk-through

| Step | What they see | Reaction |
|------|---------------|----------|
| Hero | "Start understanding your symptoms" + tagline | Good — softened vs v1. Wants a path before signup. |
| Hub #2 | 3 conditions + see all | Strong — best entry on site. |
| Credibility #3 | Bimal story | Feels like brand, not *their* story — scroll cost. |
| Stakes | Without vs with | Logical; missing "you're not imagining it." |
| Content #7 | Free guides | Late — they were googling before signup. |
| FAQ | Diagnosis question (×2) | Redundant; privacy covered elsewhere. |

### Keyword coverage

| Present | Missing / weak |
|---------|----------------|
| symptoms, bloating, IBS, GERD, understand, diagnosis, free guides | stool changes, "is this normal", anxiety, not sure what's wrong |

### Scores (v2 pre-fix)

| Dimension | Score |
|-----------|-------|
| Clarity | 5/5 |
| Validation | 3/5 |
| Starting point | 5/5 |
| Conflicting-info anxiety | 4/5 (content too late) |

### Fixes applied

- Validation band after hub (empathy beats)
- Hero secondary path → `#conditions`
- Content library moved above credibility
- Credibility demoted after proof
- FAQ deduped + "Will this diagnose me?"
